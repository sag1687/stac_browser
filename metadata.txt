La ricerca automatica interroga tutti i cataloghi STAC e mostra solo i dataset realmente presenti per quell'area, pronti per il download o per il caricamento diretto in QGIS via /vsicurl/. --- EN: Find free and open Earth-observation data by drawing a rectangle, point or line on the map, or by typing an address (Nominatim geocoding). Automatic search queries every STAC catalog and shows only datasets that actually exist for that area, ready to download or to load directly into QGIS. Catalogs: Element84 Earth Search, Microsoft Planetary Computer, USGS LandsatLook, NASA EarthData CMR, OpenLandMap, US GeoPlatform, Copernicus Data Space, Digital Earth Australia.
